What personalized sleep stories really mean

It is not the same story with a new title. It is a night-time narrative shaped around your preferences, your current mood, and the state you are bringing to bed.

How Pufflo makes stories feel personal

It remembers what you love

From ocean scenes to forests, slower pacing to softer voices, Pufflo carries preference forward.

It responds to the state of the night

Anxious nights, exhausted nights, and quiet nights do not need the same story. Pufflo can shape accordingly.

It stays fresh over time

Fixed content gets overfamiliar. Personalized stories are better suited to becoming a long-term bedtime habit.
