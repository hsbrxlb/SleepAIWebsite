What you will find here.

Clear answers, softer pacing, and a practical next step.

Why it happens

If the last thing you do is scroll, plan, or emotionally activate yourself, the mind often brings that unfinished energy straight into bed.

What sleep science suggests

The brain does not switch states instantly. Light, stimulation, and emotional charge all influence how easily it moves from alertness toward sleep.

What to do tonight

Dim lights, put away stimulating screens, stop planning tomorrow, and choose one low-pressure activity like slower breathing, a guided wind-down, or a calmer story.
