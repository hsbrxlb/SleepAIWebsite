FAQ

Questions people also ask.

Short answers for the follow-up questions that usually sit right behind the main search intent.

Can a phone track sleep without a wearable?

A bedside phone can capture useful overnight signals such as movement, breathing shifts, and room context without asking you to wear extra hardware.

What can sleep tracking without a wearable show me?

The clearest value is a simpler morning view of patterns, disruptions, and the kind of night you actually had.

Is no-wearable sleep tracking the same as clinical monitoring?

No. It is designed for everyday sleep insight and habit support, not clinical diagnosis or medical-grade monitoring.
