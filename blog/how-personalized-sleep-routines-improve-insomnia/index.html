Why it happens

Generic routines can fail because the problem is not always the same. Some nights need slower breathing, some need emotional release, and some need the mind to hand off into story or sound.

What sleep science suggests

Personalization matters because lower friction and better fit both make a routine easier to repeat. The more repeatable the routine is, the more useful it becomes for insomnia support.

What to do tonight

Build a routine around what actually helps you settle: the right light level, the right pacing, the right kind of audio, and a sequence simple enough to keep.

Where SleepAI fits

SleepAI is built around that idea of fit. Pufflo can adapt stories, tone, and the kind of support that shows up in the last part of the night. From here, continue to how to train your brain to sleep using a night routine or Can AI help you sleep better?.
