Night-time mindfulness is not daytime mindfulness

Before bed, the goal is not sharper focus. It is lower alertness, less inner friction, and more permission to slow down.

Three techniques that work better at night

1. Return attention to the breath and body weight.
2. Let thoughts pass without following them into analysis.
3. Use stable sound or story to keep attention from dropping back into anxiety loops.
