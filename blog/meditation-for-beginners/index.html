Do not overcomplicate meditation

For beginners, meditation is not about emptying the mind. It is about returning attention to the present long enough for the body to soften.

Bedtime meditation does not need to be perfect

At night, low-pressure guidance works best. If the pace is slow and the stimulation is low, it is already helping.

Where Pufflo fits in

Pufflo blends companionship, stories, and guidance so people do not have to force themselves into a meditative state alone.
