Why it happens

A lot of sleep problems are not solved by more static content. People need support that can adapt to the mood, pacing, and kind of night they are actually having.

What sleep science suggests

The useful role of AI is personalization, continuity, and lower-friction support. It should help people settle more easily, not make bigger claims than the product can actually support.

What to do tonight

Look for AI that makes bedtime feel simpler: better timing, gentler guidance, stories that stay fresh, and support that feels more responsive than a fixed track.
