A tired body can still have an alert mind

Many people reach bed physically drained but mentally active. The body is tired, but the nervous system is still scanning, looping, or holding onto the day.

Why the night can stay switched on

Stress, unresolved emotion, bright screens, and pressure to sleep can all keep alertness higher than the body wants. That is why tiredness alone does not guarantee sleep.

What helps tonight

Reduce stimulation, stop problem-solving, and move attention into something softer such as slower breathing, guided support, or a calmer story rather than forcing sleep.
