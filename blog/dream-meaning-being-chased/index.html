What being chased often points to

Dreams about being chased often reflect pressure, avoidance, unresolved worry, or the feeling that something in waking life keeps closing in.

Why the dream can repeat during stressful periods

When the day carries unfinished tension, the mind often turns it into motion, urgency, and pursuit inside the dream world. The symbol is vivid because the pressure feels unfinished.

How this links back to sleep

The dream itself matters less than the emotional load underneath it. Repeated stress dreams can be a sign that the body is not fully letting go before or during sleep.
