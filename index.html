FAQ

What is Pufflo?

Pufflo is an AI sleep companion that combines bedtime conversation, personalized stories, meditation, and lighter sleep tracking in one experience.

Can Pufflo track sleep without a wearable?

Yes. Pufflo is designed for a lower-friction bedtime setup, so you can place your phone by the bed instead of wearing extra hardware.

Is Pufflo a medical device?

No. Pufflo is designed for bedtime support, reflection, and everyday sleep insight. It is not a replacement for medical care or clinical monitoring.
