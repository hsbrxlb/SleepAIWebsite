How Pufflo understands you

See how Pufflo turns memory, context, and emotional cues into a more continuous form of night-time support.

An AI that remembers your story

It learns your preferences

Pufflo remembers the stories, pace, themes, and kind of support that help you settle more easily.

It adapts to your night rhythm

Instead of giving everyone the same template, it can move toward the style of bedtime support that fits you.

It responds to context

Real understanding comes from emotional cues and context, not just triggering the same response from the same keyword.

How that understanding builds

First, it listens

From bedtime worries to dream fragments, Pufflo collects enough context to respond with more continuity.

Then, it builds a preference model

It starts to see what calms you, what overstimulates you, and which nights need companionship more than advice.

Finally, it feeds back into every night

That understanding returns in story generation, meditation, conversation, and morning review so the experience feels connected.
