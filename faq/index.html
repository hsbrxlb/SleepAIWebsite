01 About Pufflo

About Pufflo

What Pufflo is, who it helps, and why it feels different from a meditation-first app.

What is Pufflo?

Pufflo is SleepAI's bedtime companion. It helps people settle through gentle conversation, wind-down rhythm, adaptive stories, and lighter sleep support.

Who is SleepAI for?

SleepAI is for people who feel tired but not settled: overthinkers, late-night scrollers, stressed minds, and anyone who wants the night to feel softer before sleep arrives.

How is this different from a meditation app?

Most meditation apps start with a fixed library. Pufflo feels more companion-led: story when you need story, a quieter tone when you need less, and a flow that adapts instead of replaying one script.

Does Pufflo still help if I do not fall asleep?

Yes. If Pufflo helps the night feel calmer, less lonely, or easier to rest inside, that still counts as meaningful support.

02 Sleep Support

Sleep Support

Short answers for racing thoughts, wired bodies, and waking in the middle of the night.

What can Pufflo help with at night?

Pufflo can help with racing thoughts, emotional spillover, tired-but-wired evenings, bedtime anxiety, and quieter re-entry after waking in the night.

What if I cannot stop thinking?

Pufflo can slow the moment down with prompts, story pacing, and a calmer tone that helps move attention out of active problem-solving.

What if I feel tired but still wired?

SleepAI can shift into slower breathing cues, softer pacing, and lower-effort support that helps the body downshift before sleep.

What if I wake up in the night?

The experience can become even quieter: fewer choices, shorter prompts, and less stimulation so it feels easier to settle again.

03 Trust & Boundaries

Trust & Boundaries

Clear limits, privacy expectations, and when human help matters more than a product.

Is Pufflo therapy or a medical device?

No. Pufflo is a sleep companion for bedtime support and everyday sleep reflection. It is not therapy, diagnosis, or medical monitoring.

When should I seek human help?

If someone feels unsafe, is in crisis, or needs diagnosis or treatment, they should contact emergency services, a licensed professional, or local crisis support.

How does SleepAI handle privacy?

Privacy should be explained in plain language: what is stored, how long it stays, and what controls people have. SleepAI should be clear about boundaries instead of hiding them behind vague wording.

Will Pufflo judge me for being awake again?

No. Pufflo is designed to meet people with warmth and less pressure, so the night can feel calmer instead of more shame-filled.

Boundary

Quiet support, clear boundaries.

Pufflo is here to help people slow down, not to replace professional care. For urgent mental health needs, people should still seek local emergency or licensed human support.

Support without overclaiming

SleepAI works best when it is honest about what it is: a bedtime companion built for calmer nights, not a diagnosis tool, therapist, or medical device.
