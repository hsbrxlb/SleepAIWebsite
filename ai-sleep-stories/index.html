Questions before tonight’s story.

Will the stories repeat?

Pufflo is designed to balance familiarity with variety, using your preferences and current state instead of replaying one fixed script.

Can I choose themes I like?

Yes. Theme preference is part of the experience, and clearer feedback helps Pufflo shape stories more closely around you.

Are these stories for children?

These stories are designed for adult bedtime support. A separate kids experience would need its own tone, design, and story library.
